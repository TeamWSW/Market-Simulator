How this works
This classroom model runs many randomized lifetime paths around the portfolio’s weighted characteristics. It is designed to teach uncertainty, risk, diversification, and the difference between a single path and a distribution of outcomes.

Instructor Notes

Suggested Rules

  • Minimum 8 holdings
  • Maximum 20% in one stock
  • Maximum 35% in one sector
  • Require written thesis for top positions

Suggested Grading

  • 30% thesis and stock selection logic
  • 25% diversification and risk control
  • 20% interpretation of Monte Carlo results
  • 15% benchmark comparison
  • 10% reflection on uncertainty
