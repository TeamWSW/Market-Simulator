Overview

This Stage 1 app is designed for a university personal finance or introductory investments class. Students can compare indexing with stock picking using fictional large-cap companies that resemble broad U.S. market business types.

20 fictional companies financial snapshot interactive screener portfolio builder browser-safe content

Suggested student flow

  1. Review the benchmark market and sector mix.
  2. Study company quality, valuation, margins, leverage, and news.
  3. Use the screener to narrow candidates.
  4. Build an 8–12 stock portfolio and compare it to the benchmark.

Instructor Notes

Suggested rules

  • Minimum 8 holdings
  • Maximum 20% in any one stock
  • Maximum 35% in any one sector
  • Brief memo on top 3 positions and one reason each thesis could fail

Suggested scoring

  • 30% portfolio outcome versus benchmark
  • 25% quality of investment thesis
  • 20% diversification and risk control
  • 15% use of financial and news evidence
  • 10% reflection on mistakes and lessons learned
